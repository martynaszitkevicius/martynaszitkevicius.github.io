ABOUT ME

LET'S MEET

My name is Martynas Žitkevičius, I am 25 years old. From an early age, I found myself as a very curious person who likes to observe and analyze everything around me. I always liked to observe situations, people, their behavior, problems, analyze and find the best possible solutions.

After a while, I decided to fulfill my personal analytical, critical thinking strengths, abilities and get deeper into computer science. I decided to learn coding and become a professional Front-End developer. It is a long way ahead, but the exciting and interesting one. I do like challenges, they encourage me to concentrate, think more, reveal professional growth potential, and, of course, help me gain more knowledge and experience.

I am still in the learning process, I attend online coding courses, read books, articles about current main programming, website building principles, trends, and the upcoming ones.

Personally, the logical mindset, insight, and solid life values help me to move and progress.

Moreover, I am a non-smoker, I don't drink alcohol and don't use drugs. If I want to relax, I better prefer to go on a bike trip, exercise or light some candles, turn on music, or read a good book about spiritual growth.

HOBBIES

Making time each week to do something we enjoy – and for no other reason – is beneficial for even the busiest people. Pursuing a hobby encourages us to achieve a work/life balance. Getting a short break from work and doing something we are passionate about can rejuvenate our minds and help prepare us to handle challenges in the future in a better way.

Psychology – To my mind, in order to know the world, people around you, you have to get to know yourself first. I like to analyze myself, my behaviors, strengths, weaknesses and find the best possible ways to improve them. I feel that psychology is a fascinating field that gives us knowledge and leads to personal well-being.

Philosophy – As Albert Einstein once said: "The chicken did not cross the road. The road passed beneath the chicken". Albert's statement shows that our reality's perception depends on our point of view. It is always good to see things from different perspectives to adapt and stay open-minded.

Music – I listen to music almost every day. I do really like energizing music styles as rock, as well as calming, harmonizing music: classical piano, guitar, orchestra pieces, singing bowl music.

Cycling – I do really like cycling. I think cycling is a good way to relax and stay healthy. Cycling can improve both physical and mental health and can reduce the chances of experiencing many health problems.

Traveling – I love traveling atmosphere and adventure breeze. Traveling can take us out of our daily routine and take us into new surroundings, experiences. Traveling helps to reset our body and mind.

Exercising – I try to exercise regularly. I enjoy calisthenics, running, lifting weights, swimming in the summer. Exercising helps me to relax, improve muscle strength, boost endurance. What is more, regular physical activity helps to gain self-confidence, improve mood and relax.
